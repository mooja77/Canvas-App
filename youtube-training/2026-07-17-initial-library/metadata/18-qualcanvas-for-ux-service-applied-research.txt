TITLE
QualCanvas for UX, Service and Applied Research

DESCRIPTION
Combine interviews, open-text responses, visible analysis and research memos in an applied-research workflow.

QualCanvas keeps transcripts, codes, memos and analysis views connected in a visual workspace, so the reasoning behind a qualitative interpretation remains reviewable.

Start QualCanvas: https://qualcanvas.com
Training centre: https://qualcanvas.com/training
Complete guide: https://qualcanvas.com/guide
AI use and privacy: https://qualcanvas.com/trust/ai
