TITLE
Build Cases and Compare Participants in QualCanvas

DESCRIPTION
Create analytical cases, assign source material and compare patterns across participants, sites or groups.

QualCanvas keeps transcripts, codes, memos and analysis views connected in a visual workspace, so the reasoning behind a qualitative interpretation remains reviewable.
