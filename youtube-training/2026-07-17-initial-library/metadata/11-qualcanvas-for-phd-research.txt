QualCanvas for PhD and Dissertation Research | A Defensible Analysis Trail

DESCRIPTION
See how a doctoral researcher can organise a method, analysis trail, supervision handoff and final outputs.

QualCanvas keeps transcripts, codes, memos and analysis views connected in a visual workspace, so the reasoning behind a qualitative interpretation remains reviewable.
