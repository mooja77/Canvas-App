TITLE
QualCanvas for Methods Teaching and Research Teams

DESCRIPTION
Use shared definitions, visible evidence and structured review to teach coding or coordinate a research team.

QualCanvas keeps transcripts, codes, memos and analysis views connected in a visual workspace, so the reasoning behind a qualitative interpretation remains reviewable.
