## Description

QualCanvas is a visual workspace for qualitative research. These short, practical videos show how to organise transcripts and survey responses, code passages in context, compare cases, develop a reviewable codebook, write analytical memos, curate cross-project insights, collaborate responsibly and export work for writing or handoff.

Every tutorial uses fictional demonstration material. QualCanvas supports your research process; it does not replace consent terms, ethics approval, institutional policy or researcher judgement.

Start with the beginner playlist, or use the focused tutorials to complete one outcome at a time.
