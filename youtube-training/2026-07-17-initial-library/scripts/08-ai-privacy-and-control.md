# Use AI in QualCanvas Without Giving Up Researcher Control

**Outcome:** Understand what AI features do, what data is sent and what the researcher must review and disclose.

**Audience:** Not made for kids  
**Language:** English (Ireland)  
**Voice:** en-IE-EmilyNeural

## Scene plan

### 1. AI is optional and reviewable (0:00)

- Visual: Original QualCanvas brand frame
- On-screen callout: No background analysis · no silent coding · no replacement for judgement
- Narration: QualCanvas AI features run only when you choose an AI action. They can suggest codes, draft a summary or help sketch a framework, but they do not watch the project in the background or make an interpretation final.

### 2. Read the AI use policy first (0:14)

- Visual: captures/07-trust-ai.png
- On-screen callout: Know what is sent, stored and discarded
- Narration: The AI use policy explains the request path. Relevant prompt and source text are sent through the QualCanvas backend to the selected provider for that request. QualCanvas records usage metadata, not the prompt or response content.

### 3. Choose an action deliberately (0:32)

- Visual: captures/19-ai-menu.png
- On-screen callout: Auto-code, AI code, chat and summarize are separate actions
- Narration: The AI menu keeps each action explicit. Select only the material needed for the task, review every suggestion and keep a non-AI workflow when the consent form or ethics approval does not cover external model processing.

### 4. Bring your own provider key when appropriate (0:47)

- Visual: captures/07-trust-ai.png
- On-screen callout: Provider choice and governance remain visible
- Narration: You can configure a supported provider key in account settings when your institution prefers direct provider governance. Keys are encrypted at rest, and the provider relationship still needs to fit your protocol and data-processing requirements.

### 5. Treat AI use as an ethics decision (1:03)

- Visual: captures/24-ethics.png
- On-screen callout: Data transmission, lawful basis, consent and disclosure all matter
- Narration: Sending an excerpt to a model provider is a data-processing step. Confirm lawful basis, processor terms and participant disclosure before using AI with real research data. An available button is not ethical permission.

### 6. Disclose the assistance (1:20)

- Visual: captures/20-export-menu.png
- On-screen callout: Export the AI-use record and describe the human review
- Narration: Where the study uses AI, document the provider, model, feature and human decision process. QualCanvas can export an AI disclosure record, but you should verify that the final methods statement matches the actual study.

### 7. Use AI only inside the protocol (1:37)

- Visual: Original QualCanvas brand frame
- On-screen callout: Minimise · review · document · remain accountable
- Narration: The safe pattern is minimise what you send, review every result, document the assistance and remain accountable for the interpretation. Visit qualcanvas dot com slash trust slash AI for the current policy.
