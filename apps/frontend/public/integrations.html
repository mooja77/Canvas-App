Integrations

Works with your research stack

Import transcripts from your transcription tool. Export findings to your team's documentation. No file shuffling.

Transcription import

  • Otter.aiOne-click import. Speaker labels and timestamps preserved.
  • RevDrop a Rev .docx export — speakers, timestamps, and inline notes all parsed.
  • DescriptExport from Descript as transcript .txt; QualCanvas auto-detects speaker turns.
  • Generic .docx / .txtAnything else: paste or upload, we'll parse what we can.

Export

  • Word / Google DocsTheme tables export as native Word tables. Quote-by-theme appendix one-click.
  • PDFPublication-ready theme maps. Audit trail PDFs for IRB.
  • PNG / SVGCanvas exports for slides and posters.
  • CSVCodebook export for mixed-methods triangulation with quantitative tools.

Workflow integrations

  • NotionEmbed canvases in Notion pages. Team members can view without a QualCanvas seat.
  • SlackShare-to-Slack from any canvas. Comments back into Slack thread sync to canvas.
  • Linear / JiraLink a research finding directly to a product ticket.
  • ZapierFor everything else: trigger a workflow when a new theme is created, when a study is published, etc.
