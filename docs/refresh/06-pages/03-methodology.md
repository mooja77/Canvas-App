## Chapter page anatomy

- Max measure 640px. Inter 19px body. Leading 1.7.
- H1 Fraunces 48px semibold. Chapter eyebrow `2.0 THEMATIC ANALYSIS` Inter caps 14px tracked +8%.
- No drop cap. 16px ochre rule above H1 instead.
- Sidebar TOC on desktop (sticky, ink-60% link color, ochre on current section); inline accordion on mobile.
- Pull quotes: Fraunces italic 26px, 1.5x left padding, 2px ochre vertical rule.
- Editorial asides: single inset paragraph, Fraunces italic, ochre hairlines above + below.
- Screenshots inline, full-measure, captioned in Inter italic 14px with ochre dot prefix.
- Bottom: prev/next nav (View Transitions API). Plus one discreet `Try this in QualCanvas →` CTA.
- Target 1,800–2,500 words per chapter. Chapter outlines in [08 §8.5](../08-content-strategy.md).
