**Intent.** A page Maya can email to her advisor. No competitor has this — see [01 §1.3](../01-strategic-positioning.md) moat #5.

## Content

- Eyebrow `CITATION`.
- H1 Fraunces: `How to cite QualCanvas.`
- Sub (Inter 19px): `Pick a format. Copy. Paste into your manuscript.`
- One short paragraph: _"If QualCanvas is part of your method, please cite us. Here's how."_
- Tabs: `BibTeX · APA · Chicago · RIS` — copy-to-clipboard on each.
- BibTeX includes `doi` and `version` fields so citations remain reproducible.

## Layout

Single page, no chrome. Tabs sit below the headline; selected tab is ink-900 with ochre underline (see [04 §4.9](../04-brand-design-system.md) Citation tab states).
